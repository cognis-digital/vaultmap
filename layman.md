Vaultmap is a personal inventory tool that helps you keep track of all your financial accounts, assets, and important records — bank accounts, investments, retirement funds, crypto, real estate, insurance policies, and more — in a single encrypted file only you can open. When something happens to you, your family or executor can get a clear picture of everything you own and owe without hunting through paperwork. You run it from the command line: create a vault, add entries with values and beneficiary names, and get an estate-planning summary showing your net worth and which entries are still missing critical information. It is designed for individuals who want a private, self-hosted record that no cloud service can access.
